Contextual analysis of the marburg virus disease with Natural Language Processing. Understanding the disease and its symptoms. Data was scrapped from WHO's website and analysed with Natural Language Processing methods.

Introduction

Why is the marburg virus disease important?

The virus kills an average of half of the people it infects, but the most harmful strains have killed up to 88%. Recovered persons can infect others via blood or semen for months after contracting Marburg. Even postmortem, the body of an infected individual can be contagious. Given how harmful the virus is, it follows that we must take precautions to avoid getting infected. However, a lot of individuals are unaware of this virus or only know a little about it. An outbreak of the disease occurred in the Ashanti Region resulting in the death of 2 people and the quarantine of 98 individuals. As a result, it is imperative that we use web scraping to deliver the required information.

Contextual Data Analysis

Data was analysed to extract keywords and some named-entities like animals and names of locations. The keywords give a general idea about the MVD and some common ways of contracting the disease from other people The symptoms of the MVD were also generated from the sentences in the text.

Abstract Generation

Abstractive summary was performed on the data. This was done by creating a DataFrame with the sentences in the text and the corresponding length. The sentences were sorted according to their length and the top 5 sentences were selected to summarize the whole chunk of text.
